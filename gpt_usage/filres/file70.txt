Overview
Mission
Our mission is to cultivate a flourishing ecosystem of happy tokens and traders. We list great tokens faster than any other exchange and use the tokens to reward learning and discovering the next bitcoins before others. So the value and enjoyment of trading on our platform is growing.
Product and Activities
We offer exchange listing and marketing activities such as airdrops, giveaways, and trading competitions. These activities use token budgets to reward community engagement through tasks like creating and distributing creatives that explain new cryptocurrencies, sharing tweets, and inviting friends to join. You can initiate the launch of new activity types if there’s a scalable demand. We focus on the great tokens—so that our community earns both social currency (the respect of peers for sharing notable tokens and learning about new technologies) and monetary currency (from tokens appreciating in value).
Focus on Quality Tokens
We concentrate on tokens that have substantial intrinsic value, including those with trending narratives in 2024 such as layer 1 and 2 technologies, zk, liquid restacking, memes, and gaming. Our selection also prioritizes tokens with a large community base driving adoption and tokens from teams who communicate their value and marketing strategy effectively. As a manager, you are expected to become an expert in these areas, gaining the trust of projects and encouraging more activities.
Selling Techniques and CRM Process
After qualification to focus on the great token, building strong rapport is the first step in understanding client needs and effectively presenting our activities as solutions. We avoid the pitfalls of aggressive selling which is typically pursued by less skilled managers and results in low-quality projects and disengaged communities. You will learn essential sales scripts but are also encouraged to develop a unique selling style that aligns with your personality—whether as a discerning expert who strategically navigates projects or as an enthusiastic advocate passionately pushing for maximum support from Latoken.
Team Management and CRM Utilization
You grow to perfection due to intense work. There no option to be just good enough. You must grow and you leverage CRM to keep you and your environment disciplined for the intense work. CRM helps you to manage your portfolio, keeping it well-organized and transparent with up-to-date contacts and communications. From almost day one, you’ll be involved in hiring, setting the tone for transparency, intense work, and meticulous diligence within your team.
Key Metrics and Incentives
Your primary metrics include the number of traders acquired and the efficiency of using budgets for real tasks that engage actual participants, leading to low client acquisition costs (CAC). These budgets are determined by the number of activities multiplied by the average budget per activity. Daily diligent CRM work is essential to move prospects from initial contact to active engagement.
Acquired Traders = Budget / CAC. Budget = Activities X Average Budget.
Incentives align with your achievements in user acquisition and activity launches. Bonuses are also awarded for the successful onboarding of new hires and closing listings with sales managers. Additionally, your ability to solve clients' problems contributes to your metrics, enhancing your potential for bonuses tied to nurturing hot leads.

Listings

Primary Market/IEO: Allows projects to sell tokens directly to the public, primarily through Initial Exchange Offerings (IEO), bypassing intermediaries for direct engagement.
Secondary Market: Opening pair (normally against USDT) in our order book. This allows traders to buy and sell tokens among themselves.
Launches are supported by the marketing activities designed to attract traders to the new tokens, coupled with market-making to minimize slippage and ensure smooth trading experiences. Clients and sales managers can select the most appropriate service package  at Latoken Listing Packages.

	Initial Contact and Rapport
Begin with a succinct introduction as a new growth manager dedicated to enhancing their token's market presence. If there is no initial response, follow up by highlighting specific marketing advantages of their project. Focus on building rapport and trust without jumping to conclusions, and aim to secure a meeting to discuss their marketing plan in detail.
Learn Needs
Endeavor to obtain their marketing plan either in written form or through a detailed discussion. Ask about their views on using the token as a marketing budget, highlighting the innate scalability of crypto as a marketing tool. Use open-ended questions to explore their thoughts on airdrops, and empathize with any concerns about price impacts. Acknowledge the common issue of bot-driven price dumps but explain that with effective bot filtering and rewarding genuine users, the risk of sell pressure decreases significantly. Highlight that real users are more likely to hold tokens long-term, fostering organic growth.
Clarify Solutions
Clearly articulate that their token can serve as both a marketing budget and channel, with robust measures in place to protect against bot exploitation. Emphasize the importance of careful campaign management to ensure that marketing efforts are not wasted and are directed towards meaningful engagement.
Closing the Deal
Convey urgency in decision-making, not as a pressure tactic but as a natural component of timing in the market. Illustrate the opportunity with a specific listing slot available the following week, stressing that such prime opportunities are scarce and typically reserved quickly. Mention that this slot can only be held for 24 hours before another manager might claim it for a different client, underscoring the need for prompt action.

Формат репорта
Репорт делается в виде поста в слеке каждый вечер для демонстрации на утреннем стендапе, чтобы команде были видны проблемы и решения. В конце недели, но не позже Вс, делается еженедельный репорт для стендапа в Пн в 9:00.
Движение по воронке (указать период: неделя 30.06 - 2.07):
APPLIED XX/YY -> HOT XX/YY -> INTERVIEW XX/YY -> OFFER DRAFT XX/YY -> OFFER SENT XX/YY -> TESTW YY
Активность за день:
Задачи Cделанные XX | Просроченные YY
Чаты Проактивные XX | Просроченные YY
Звонки XX | Дозвоны YY | Успешные ZZ
